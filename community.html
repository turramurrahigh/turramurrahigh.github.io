Our Students in the Community

Students at Turramurra High School are active participants in our local and wider community. As a high profile public school, we value our community partnerships and recognize that our students are a reflection and product of our community.

Our students are involved in numerous community projects each year including the Salvation Army Red Shield Appeal, Premier's Volunteering program, and the Turramurra branch of Bendigo Bank Youth Leadership project.

Our school has a long tradition of supporting three key charity organisations; Red Kite, the Muscular Dystrophy Association and the Leukemia Foundation.

In recent years, we have established global community connections with a community in Northern India where we are contributing to the building of a high school.

Students of Turramurra High School recognize the value of citizenship and we are very proud of the contribution that our students make to our local community.
